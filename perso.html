CHARACTER GENERATOR

Welcome to the character generator.

Here you are, in the forge of gods ! You're about to create your incarnation for the gaming sessions. Just follow this few steps :

  • FIRST, you'll have to choose a picture and set your name. (the gender is only needed for the name generator's. It has no impact on the game.)
  • SECOND, you can choose a class. It will define your natural skills and a double bonus on your stats. If you don't want any kind of help, you can choose "Apatride", that way, you'll got only a Chc small bonus (you gonna need it ! Trust me...).
  • THIRD, you can choose a tribe. It will grant you with a specific education bonus. On the other hand, you'll get a malus on an opposite skill (nobody's perfect).
  • FOURTH, you'll have to set none but 6 differents skills that you'll use on your journey. To understand each and every skills capabilities, go HERE first. (hover the skill's name for a little reminder)
  • FIFTH, and before create your next incarnation, check the fighting skills and Transcendence ability. They'll determine your fighting style. They can't be change ! Be careful !
  • LAST, click "Create" to give life to your avatar ! Self-generated skills, as luck, starting gold and destiny Pt will be set here.
